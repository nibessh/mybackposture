Central Daylight Time (CDT) is the second easternmost time zone in the United States and is also used in Canada. It covers all or parts of 20 states in the US and three provinces or territories in Canada . About a third of the population in the USA live in the CDT time zone. It spans from northern Canada and south to Mexico.
